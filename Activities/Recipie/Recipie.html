Cheese Cake

Ingredients

  1. Crust
    • Graham crackers / Digestive biscuits (crushed)
    • Butter (melted)
    • Sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  2. Cheesecake Filling
    • Cream cheese (softened)
    • Sugar (granulated or powdered)
    • Eggs
    • Sour cream / Fresh cream / Yogurt (for smoothness)
    • Vanilla extract
  3. Optional Toppings / Flavoring
    • Fresh fruits or fruit compote
    • Chocolate / Caramel sauce
    • Whipped cream
    • Nuts or cookie crumbs

Recipie

  1. Prepare the Crust
    • Crush graham crackers/digestive biscuits into fine crumbs.
    • Mix with melted butter (and a little sugar if you like).
    • Press mixture firmly into the bottom of a springform pan.
    • Chill in the fridge for 15-20 mins to set.
  2. Make the filling
    • Beat cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
    • Add sugar and mix well.
    • Mix in eggs one at a time.
    • Add sour cream/fresh cream and vanilla extract, blend until smooth.
  3. Bake
    • Pour the filling over the prepared crust.
    • Place the pan in a water bath (optional, prevents cracks).
    • Bake at 160°C (325°F) for about 50–60 mins, until edges are set but center is slightly jiggly.
  4. Cool
    • Turn off the oven, leave cheesecake inside with door slightly open for 1 hour (prevents cracking).
    • Then refrigerate for at least 4–6 hours (best overnight).
